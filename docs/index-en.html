Abstract

This ontology was created within the scope of the DEDALUS project to enhance interoperability among various services and pilots

Introduction back to ToC

The DEDALUS ontology encompasses all the information that needs to be managed by the various services, technology enablers, and pilots within the DEDALUS project. The project aims to empower residential consumers to engage in adaptable, technology-driven energy management solutions. As such, this ontology focuses on demand response, buildings, and users, enabling the decoupling of infrastructure from software services and applications.

DEDALUS ontology: Description back to ToC

The DEDALUS ontology organizes and centralizes all the essential information required by the services, technological enablers, and pilots involved in the DEDALUS project. By doing so, the project seeks to empower residential consumers, fostering their participation in flexible and technology-based energy management solutions. This ontology places particular emphasis on demand response, building systems, and user interactions, facilitating the separation of physical infrastructure from software services and applications.
